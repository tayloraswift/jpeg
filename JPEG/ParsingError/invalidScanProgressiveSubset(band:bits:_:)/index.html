Enumeration Case

invalidScanProgressiveSubset(band:bits:_:)

A scan header specified an invalid progressive frequency band or bit range given the image coding process.

Declaration

case invalidScanProgressiveSubset(band:(Int, Int), bits:(Int, Int), JPEG.Process)

Associated values

band

The lower and upper bounds of the frequency band read from the scan header.

bits

The lower and upper bounds of the bit range read from the scan header.

_

The coding process used by the image.
