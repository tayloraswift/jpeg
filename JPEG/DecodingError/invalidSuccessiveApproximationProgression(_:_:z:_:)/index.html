Enumeration Case

invalidSuccessiveApproximationProgression(_:_:z:_:)

A scan did not follow the correct successive approximation sequence for at least one frequency coefficient.

Declaration

case invalidSuccessiveApproximationProgression(Range<Int>, Int, z:Int, JPEG.Component.Key)

Associated values

_

The bit range encoded by the scan.

_

The index of the least-significant bit encoded so far for the coefficient z.

z

The zigzag index of the coefficient.

_

The component key of the invalidated color channel.

Overview

Successive approximation must refine bits starting from the most-significant and going towards the least-significant, only the initial scan for each coefficient can encode more than one bit at a time.
